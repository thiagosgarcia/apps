Mechanical Calculator

Mechanical Calculator is a modern Android calculator inspired by classic printing machines. It keeps a running paper tape of your work, adds a tactile print-style feel, and lets you export or share results in multiple formats.

Privacy policy Terms and conditions Back to showcase

Why it stands out

  • Paper tape history records entries, operations, and results like a receipt.
  • Optional print animation and sound effects add a mechanical calculator feel.
  • Landscape mode shows the tape and keypad side by side for easier reviewing.
  • Users can export the tape as TXT, CSV, PNG, JPG, or PDF.

Best fit for

Students, home users, and anyone who wants a calculator that keeps visible working history instead of acting like a plain single-line keypad.

It is especially useful when checking calculations, teaching arithmetic, or sharing a record of what was entered.

Privacy snapshot

  • No account is required.
  • Calculations and tape history stay on the device.
  • Optional ads use Google AdMob and can be disabled in Settings.
  • Exports are generated locally before the Android share sheet is opened.
