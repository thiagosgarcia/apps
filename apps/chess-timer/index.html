Chess Timer

Need a clean chess clock that does not get in the way of the game? Chess Timer keeps turn changes obvious, setup simple, and the whole board session smoother for both players.

Privacy policy Terms and conditions Back to all apps

Why you'll like it

  • Two-player layout that is easy to read across the board.
  • Preset time controls plus fully custom hours, minutes, and seconds.
  • Pause, resume, restart, and clear turn-state feedback.
  • Optional auto-pause when the app is backgrounded or closed.

Great when you want to...

  • Start a blitz, rapid, or casual game in seconds.
  • Hand the turn over clearly with one tap after each move.
  • Personalize the look with theme color and sound settings.
  • Use a layout that still feels comfortable on tablets and in landscape.

Privacy at a glance

  • No accounts or sign-in flow.
  • Player names and settings stay on the device.
  • No third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are integrated.
  • Optional banner ads may appear through Google AdMob and can be disabled.
