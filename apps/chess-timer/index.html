Chess Timer

Chess Timer is a fast, distraction-free two-player clock for over-the-board games. It focuses on clear turn handoff, flexible timing presets, and a layout that feels natural on phones and tablets.

Privacy policy Terms and conditions Back to showcase

Core features

  • Two-player layout that is easy to read across the board.
  • Preset time controls plus fully custom hours, minutes, and seconds.
  • Pause, resume, restart, and clear turn-state feedback.
  • Optional auto-pause when the app is backgrounded or closed.

Presentation and usability

  • Optional sound effects for taps and time expiration.
  • Theme color picker for a personalized table-side look.
  • Landscape mode makes the timer more comfortable on tablets.
  • Simple, low-friction UI built to stay out of the way of the game.

Privacy snapshot

  • No accounts or sign-in flow.
  • Player names and settings stay on the device.
  • No third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are integrated.
  • Optional banner ads may appear through Google AdMob and can be disabled.
