Columns Classic

Looking for a quick puzzle fix? Columns Classic gives you fast falling-block action, colorful chain combos, and plenty of high-score energy without needing an online connection.

Privacy policy Terms and conditions Back to all apps Coming soon

Why you'll like it

  • Match 3 or more blocks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Choose from 4 difficulty levels and 3 board sizes.
  • Enjoy 4 visual styles for pieces, including square and diamond variants.
  • Chain reactions reward smart setups and fast decision-making.

Great when you want to...

  • Jump into a quick score chase without learning a complicated system.
  • Play offline on the train, on the couch, or anywhere else.
  • Use gesture controls that feel quick and responsive.
  • Fine-tune the feel with optional buttons, sensitivity controls, and seasonal mode.

Privacy at a glance

  • The game is designed to work offline.
  • Settings, saved game state, and high scores are stored locally on the device.
  • Ads are optional and handled through Google AdMob when enabled.
  • No account system or developer-run backend is required.
