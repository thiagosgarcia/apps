Columns Classic

Columns Classic is a quick, offline falling-block puzzle game built around rotating colors, clearing 3+ matches, and chasing chain-reaction scores across multiple levels and grid sizes.

Privacy policy Terms and conditions Back to showcase

Gameplay highlights

  • Match 3 or more blocks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Choose from 4 difficulty levels and 3 board sizes.
  • Enjoy 4 visual styles for pieces, including square and diamond variants.
  • Chain reactions reward smart setups and fast decision-making.

Controls and customization

  • Tap or swipe up to rotate the column colors.
  • Swipe left or right to move across the board.
  • Swipe down or double-tap to drop faster.
  • Optional on-screen buttons, sensitivity controls, and seasonal mode.

Privacy snapshot

  • The game is designed to work offline.
  • Settings, saved game state, and high scores are stored locally on the device.
  • Ads are optional and handled through Google AdMob when enabled.
  • No account system or developer-run backend is required.
