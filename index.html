Mechanical Calculator

If you like seeing every step, this calculator keeps a receipt-style paper tape of your work and makes it easy to save or share the result.

  • Optional printing animation and sound effects
  • Landscape layout with configurable keypad position

Columns Classic

When you want a quick arcade-style brain break, Columns Classic gives you fast falling-block action, chain combos, and plenty of high-score energy.

  • 4 difficulty levels and 3 grid sizes
  • Horizontal, vertical, and diagonal matches
  • Gesture controls plus optional on-screen buttons
  • Separate top-20 leaderboards by mode and grid size

Chess Timer

For over-the-board games, this chess clock keeps turns clear, setup easy, and the whole session smoother for both players.

  • Fast turn handoff for two-player over-the-board games
  • Preset time controls plus custom hours, minutes, and seconds
  • Pause, resume, restart, and optional auto-pause
  • Theme color picker, sounds, and optional landscape mode
