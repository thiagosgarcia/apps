Utility

Mechanical Calculator

If you like seeing every step, this app keeps a receipt-style paper tape for calculations, then adds built-in tools for conversions, interest scenarios, and splitting a bill.

  • Paper tape memory that records entries and results
  • Calculator with receipt-style tape display
  • Bill Splitter tool with even/uneven distribution
  • Unit Converter for common measurements
  • Interest Calculator with multiple calculation modes
  • Random Generator for number sequences
  • Exports for calculator and tool results in TXT, CSV, PNG, JPG, and PDF
  • Optional printing effects and landscape keypad controls

Columns Classic

When you want a quick arcade-style brain break, Columns Classic gives you fast falling-block action, chain combos, and plenty of high-score energy.

  • 4 difficulty levels and 3 grid sizes
  • Horizontal, vertical, and diagonal matches
  • Gesture controls plus optional on-screen buttons
  • Separate top-20 leaderboards by mode and grid size

Chess Timer

For over-the-board games, this chess clock keeps turns clear, setup easy, and the whole session smoother for both players.

  • Fast turn handoff for two-player over-the-board games
  • Preset time controls plus custom hours, minutes, and seconds
