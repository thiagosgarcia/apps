Utility

Mechanical Calculator

An educational Android calculator inspired by classic printing calculators, complete with a paper tape history and export tools.

  • Paper tape memory that records entries and results
  • TXT, CSV, PNG, JPG, and PDF export and sharing
  • Optional printing animation and sound effects
  • Landscape layout with configurable keypad position
App page Privacy Terms

Game

Columns Classic

A fast, offline falling-block puzzle game built around color matching, chain reactions, gesture controls, and score chasing.

  • 4 difficulty levels and 3 grid sizes
  • Horizontal, vertical, and diagonal matches
  • Gesture controls plus optional on-screen buttons
  • Separate top-20 leaderboards by mode and grid size
App page Privacy Terms

Tool

Chess Timer

A focused two-player chess clock with presets, custom time controls, sound settings, and a layout that works well at the board.

  • Fast turn handoff for two-player over-the-board games
  • Preset time controls plus custom hours, minutes, and seconds
  • Pause, resume, restart, and optional auto-pause
  • Theme color picker, sounds, and optional landscape mode
